Novel

Frankenstein

When I looked at all the books we would be reading senior year for Euro Lit, never did I imagine that Frankenstein would be my favorite novel of the year. I think what truly connected me to the novel / understand it's real life implications/lessons was the research project we did around it. It allowed me to delve into Artificial Intelligence (which I've always loved reading about) and truly understand how it worked + how it could be similar of a predicament to Frankenstein, where technology made simply to make "advanced tech" could potentially turn against us. This was honestly such an intereseting unit, and I thank you for giving us the opportunity to research it :)
